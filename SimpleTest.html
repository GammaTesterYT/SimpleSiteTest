Catalog of Brown Dwarfs

The catalog of brown dwarfs is an website made to show parameters and information about brown dwarfs and ultra-cool dwarfs. It use primarily IOP science, exoplanet.eu, NASA exoplanet archive and mathematical equations as it sources. It was programmed with HTML inside GitHub. Brown dwarf are massive and cool semi stellar objects that are heavier and bigger than planets but lighter and smaller than stars. They have a mass ranging from 13 to 80 jupiter masses
